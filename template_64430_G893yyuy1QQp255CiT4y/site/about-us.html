Welcome to Our Taxi

Luxury Taxi was founded in 1994 by Ronald Chen, a former taxi driver and current entrepreneur to revolutionize the industry of city transportation.

As a company that operates throughout the USA with a vast and developed partner network, we provide taxicab services in big cities, at many central airports, and other locations. Our service range is constantly developing, and that’s one of the reasons why people love us.

local routes

Looking for an affordable and easy way to reach another part of the city or state? Don’t worry, we’ve got you covered with our high-quality local taxi services.

abroad travels

We provide not only airport transfer services for international travelers but also a wide variety of supplemental taxi services abroad.

business travels

Our large capacity, timeliness and great reputation make us your ideal partner for professional and comfortable business travel. We can also make a good impression with your clients.

24/7 online support

We provide a variety of payment methods that include Paypal, Visa/Mastercard/American Express and lots of others, which can be customized.

our history

April, 2016
We gathered the team of dedicated and knowledgeable drivers and dispatchers

Before we started working at Luxury Taxi as a team, we extended our team with young, talented, and promising drivers and dispatchers who later became the foundation of our company.

September, 2016
Opening new offices and service improvement

To introduce our services to new clients, we opened our first New York office in 2008. It instantly showed great results. Our fleet increased by 20 cars and our team welcomed a lot of new drivers.

March, 2017
International cooperation with other taxi service providers

As we continue to grow, develop, and expand, we started a special partnership program aimed at cooperation with our overseas colleagues. We already offer our services in Germany and France.
