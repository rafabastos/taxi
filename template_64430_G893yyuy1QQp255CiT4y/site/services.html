Need a ride?

Wherever you need to go, Luxury Taxi is ready to take you there, day or night. Our fleet of 600 taxis provides service throughout the USA and Europe. Our helpful, friendly drivers are very familiar with the area, so we can handle all your travel needs.

We provide professional and seasoned taxi services to guests and residents of the USA and Europe.

Whether you are looking for an airport shuttle service or just need a taxi to quickly reach a local destination, our team of dispatchers will find a driver and a taxi for you.

our advantages

Local Routes

Looking for an affordable and easy way to reach another part of the city or state? Don’t worry, we’ve got you covered with our local taxi services.

Abroad Travels

We provide not only airport transfer services for international travelers but also a wide variety of supplemental taxi services abroad.

Business Travels

Our large capacity, timeliness and great reputation make us your ideal partner for professional and comfortable business travel.

24/7 Online Support

We provide a variety of payment methods that include Paypal, Visa/Mastercard/American Express and lots of others, which can be customized.

As-Directed service

As flexible at it is reliable, our As-Directed hourly service puts a chauffeur at your disposal for as long as necessary. No destination is required to book it.
