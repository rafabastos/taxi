Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pre-book a cab?

You can pre-book a cab through our website or by calling our office at toll free number which you can find on our website. Or simply download our App on your Android phone or iPhone - it is the easiest way to book a cab.

How can I order a taxi?

Ordering our taxi is easy! Since you are already here you can book your trip now using our website. Ordering a taxi online saves you time, because the details of your trip are automatically entered. Or you can book a taxi on your smartphone or mobile device, using our booking app.

How can I pay for my transfer in the taxi?

We accept cash and major credit cards - Visa, MasterCard, and American Express.

How do I recognize your taxis?

All our taxis are dark yellow and they have our logo clearly displayed both on the bonnet and on the sides. On the lantern you should look for a yellow circle in the middle off the word TAXI. If you need additional information about our taxis and services, contact us.
